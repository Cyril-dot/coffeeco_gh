Brewing Equipment

Explore our premium selection of coffee brewing tools, from state-of-the-art espresso machines to elegant French presses. Perfect for every coffee enthusiast.

La Marzocco Linea Mini

Description: A professional espresso machine in a compact size, renowned for its precision and reliability.

Usage Tips: Preheat the machine for at least 30 minutes for consistent temperature. Clean the portafilter regularly for optimal performance.

Breville Bambino Plus

Description: Compact yet powerful, offering a strong espresso shot perfect for beginners.

Usage Tips: Ensure the grind size is fine enough for espresso extraction. Clean the steam wand after every use.

Bodum Chambord French Press

Description: A classic French press that produces a rich, full-bodied cup of coffee.

Usage Tips: Use coarse ground coffee. Let steep for 4 minutes before pressing down the plunger to avoid bitterness.

Chemex Coffee Maker

Description: A beautifully designed pour-over coffee maker that produces a clean, crisp cup of coffee with enhanced clarity.

Usage Tips: Use a medium-fine grind and pour water slowly in a spiral motion for an even extraction. Pre-wet the filter to prevent paper taste.

Aeropress Coffee Maker

Description: A versatile and compact brewing tool that offers a smooth and rich coffee concentrate, great for espresso-style drinks or regular coffee.

Usage Tips: Experiment with brew times and coffee-to-water ratios to find your preferred taste. Press slowly for optimal extraction.

Hario V60 Dripper

Description: A pour-over device that allows baristas to manually control the brewing process for a clean cup.

Usage Tips: Use a gooseneck kettle for precision pouring and pre-wet the filter to remove any papery taste.
